Our Story

A Table Born
from the Sea

Nestled on the shore of Theologos, Anthos is more than a restaurant — it is a feeling. Tables steps from the water, salt breeze in the air, and a kitchen that turns the day's freshest catch into something you'll remember for years.

Every dish is made with locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients — crafted with care and plated with pride. Every meal ends with a complimentary surprise from the kitchen, because hospitality here is not a gesture, it's a way of life.

Regulars call it a destination in itself. Visitors say it's the best meal of their trip. We simply call it home.
